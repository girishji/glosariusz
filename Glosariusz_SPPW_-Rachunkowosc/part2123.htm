Shareholders’ Equity • Kapitał własny • An entity’s net worth that is composed of original investment, unrealised profits, various reserves and retained earnings. The book value of shareholders’ equity is measured at any one time by the balance sheet. The mar- ket value of shareholders’ equity is measured by the market capitalisation of outstanding shares. See also net asset value.
