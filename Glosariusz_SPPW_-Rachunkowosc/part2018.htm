Progressive Income Taxes • Progresywny podatek dochodowy • A system of personal income taxation that gradually increases marginal tax rates of taxation – the “tax bite” – as taxable income rises. While cynics call it “soak the rich” it is based on the economic logic of diminishing marginal utility: the more we have of something, includ- ing income, the less valuable it is. Assuming this is the case, then taxing marginal income at higher amounts should be less “painful”.
