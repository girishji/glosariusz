Quants are the most prevalent in modern finance, especially in the derivatives and risk management fields. Computers have liberated them. Accountants do arithmetic; quants do math. While their analytical skills are impressive, there is a lingering concern that their arcane arts can usurp common sense and sound business judgement (see below). See also rocket scientists.
