Risk Capital • Kapitał ryzyka • A broad term for any source of financing – typically equity – that is prepared to incur high risks in exchange for the pro- mise of high returns (say 30%+). For example, it could be deployed in a business start-up, a restructuring or a special transaction such as a leveraged buyout. Hedge funds, private equity and venture capitalists are the typical providers.
