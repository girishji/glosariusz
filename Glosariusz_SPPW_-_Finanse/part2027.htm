Rising Star (vernacular) • Wschodząca gwiazda (żargon) • A company with very favourable performance and future prospects that was previously in the dol- drums. A turning point is getting an upgrade in its credit rating that takes it from speculative to investment grade status. It is the opposite of a fallen angel.
