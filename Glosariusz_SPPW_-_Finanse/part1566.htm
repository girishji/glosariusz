Flip Transaction (slang) • Transakcja wirtualna (slang) • Any transaction which is quickly reversed bringing the parties back to their original financial position. Flips are typically a buy quickly followed by a sell. For example, a spec- ulative investor may be said to have “flipped the property”: instead of holding real estate for a long term gain or usage, the property is immediately sold for a quick profit. Flips are often motivated by profit seeking, income tax, legal or even money laundering reasons to give an impression of commercial activity. Flips may be fundamentally artificial when measured by the substance over form criteria. See also churning.
