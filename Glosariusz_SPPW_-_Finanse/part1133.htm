People Empowerment

Instead of an aristocracy of the merely rich, we are moving to an aristocracy of the capable and the rich. The financial revolution is opening the gates of the aristocratic clubs to everyone. In this respect, the financial revolution is thoroughly liberal in spirit. Instead of capital, it puts the human being at the centre of economic activity because, when capital is freely available, it is skills, ideas, hard work, and inescapably, luck that create wealth.

The Financial Revolution and Individual Freedom excerpted from Saving Capitalism from the Capitalists (2003) by R. Rajan & L. Zingales, p. 92
