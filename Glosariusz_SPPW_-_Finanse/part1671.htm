Internal Growth Rate • IGR • Wewnętrzna stopa wzrostu • The maxi- mum annual growth rate in sales that a company can achieve without resorting to external financing (either debt or equity). Thus the new investments in work- ing capital or fixed assets required to support a higher level of sales will be fi- nanced entirely by a firm’s own earnings. As a consequence, a firm’s leverage will be reduced at the internal growth rate since the total amount of equity is grow- ing but not total debt.‌
