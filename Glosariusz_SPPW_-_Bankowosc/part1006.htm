Quantitative Models • Modele ilościowe – Mathematical simulation of a bank’s overall exposure to risk, particularly market risk from trading activities and derivatives. Powerful computer technology is allowing information to be aggregated and analysed for risk management purposes. To be useful to bank management, quantitative models must be both comprehensive (capture all rel- evant exposures) and comprehensible (distil complex data down to an overall risk profile of the bank). See also value at risk and stress testing.
