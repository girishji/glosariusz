Trade Date vs. Settlement Date Accounting • Księgowanie transakcji według daty zawarcia lub daty rozliczenia • The accounting choice available in

selecting the time to value securities purchases or sales: on the day they were traded or when cash payment is settled. Because of the 2–3 day lag between the two dates, market prices may differ. Most users prefer the trade date, even if a mark-to-market gain/loss has to be recognised in the books.

Trading Account (UK) • Rachunek operacyjny (Wielka Brytania) • A section in the income statement/profit and loss statement that is devoted to “trading activities”

i.e. the buying and selling of the enterprise’s goods or services in the normal course of its business. The term is most commonly found in the UK. Elsewhere, the distinction from the rest of the income statement is not made.
