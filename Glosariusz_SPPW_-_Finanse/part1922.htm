Portfolio Shifts • Przebudowa portfela • Changes in the composition of an investment portfolio over time due to alterations in risk-reward relationships or market trends. For example, private investors are tending to move into stock market via mutual funds and of low yielding money market funds. In banking, we can often see a shift out of government securities into other asset classes.

Position, To • Pozycja wyjściowa • To hold forth the hope of achieving future results based on being prepared in the present. A company might say that they are “well positioned to take advantage of trends in the market.” The notion can have a defensive connotation as in “sorry we haven’t quite delivered yet but give us time”.
