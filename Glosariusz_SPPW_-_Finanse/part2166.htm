This equation perhaps just formalises the obvious: businesses not only want to make money but as much of it and as soon as they can. The obvious simplicity of the theory covers some deeper complexities. For example, total revenues are a function of sales volumes, prices, marketing strategies, distribution channels; total costs are a function of production volumes, inputs, technology, scale econ- omies, etc; discount rates are driven by capital structure, leverage and financial markets; strategy, organisation structure, staffing, government regulations will effect them all.
