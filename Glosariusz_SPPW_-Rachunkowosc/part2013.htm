Profit Margin • Marża zysku • The amount of profit/income earned as percentage of sales revenue. Profit is usually defined as gross profit on individual items of inventory in which case it is referred to as gross margin. In different contexts, profit margin can refer to net profit or net income as a percentage of sales in which case it is “net margin”.
