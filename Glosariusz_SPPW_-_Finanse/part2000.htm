Relevance/Relevant Cash Flow • Istotność/Istotny przepływ pieniężny • The criteria used to determine what information should be factored into a de- cision. Relevance is closely related to economist’s optimizing rule at the mar- gin of time (i.e. at any point in time going forward): to maximize profit, set production output where margin revenue equals marginal cost. In capital ex- penditure budgeting, the relevance issue is very important. Only those things impacted by the decision (i.e. that have an impact on the future) should be considered. Accordingly, while opportunity cost has relevance, sunk costs have no relevance.
