Horses on the Payroll 935‌‌


Horizontal Equity • Sprawiedliwość pozioma • One of the criteria in fairness of taxation: taxpayers with a similar ability to pay taxes should pay the same amount. See also vertical equity.

Horses on the Payroll (slang) • Zatrudniać martwe dusze (żargon) • An infor- mal expression for the fraud of non-existent people being created, being put on a payroll and then being paid. The perpetrators then take the cash. The idea of a horse comes from the common practice of giving them human-sounding names. You don’t name a rat.
