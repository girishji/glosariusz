Fixed Costs • Koszty stałe • Expenses that are independent of production volumes and which cannot be modified in the short run without radically altering the business. Examples include:

◆ factory depreciation or premises rental,

◆ labour payroll,

◆ sales, general and administrative expenses,

◆ interest on outstanding debt.
