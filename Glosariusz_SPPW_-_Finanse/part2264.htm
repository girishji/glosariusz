Yankee Bond (vernacular) • Obligacja jankeska (żargon) • A bond issued in the US capital markets by a non-US borrower, most often a Canadian entity. It is a similar notion as a Samurai bond or a Bull Dog bond.


Yearling (UK slang) • Roczniak (slang brytyjski) • A bond with a term-to- maturity of one year or less. They can be issued by local government authorities in Britain. Its name derives from cattle that are less than one year old.
