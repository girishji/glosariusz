Unlike financial ratios that are tools of analysis, EPS are strictly defined calculations under accounting standards. EPS is one of the most widely used statistics in the stock market and is a key driver of share prices.

Earnings Quality (vernacular) • Jakość zysku (żargon) • The qualitative attributes of net profits in terms of how reliable they are as predictors of financial performance. Earnings quality has two distinctive sources:
