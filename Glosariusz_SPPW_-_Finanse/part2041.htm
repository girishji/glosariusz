Run the Numbers 1195‌

Run the Numbers (slang) • Analiza liczbowa (slang) • To check the com- mercial merits of a business proposition by quantifying the amounts involved in investing or profiting. This might involve a budget or a forecast. Thus, a manager might say: “It sounds like an interesting idea; let’s run the numbers to see how much it would actually cost”. Or, an analyst might say: “Until we run the num- bers on various scenarios, our earnings per share estimates are unclear”. The provenance of the term is related to the “running” of computer programs.
