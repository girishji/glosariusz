Commercial leases provide a long term, stable source of income. In the case of residential apartments, REITS can be counter-cyclical to the private homeowner’s market as “bad home owners become good renters”. Real Estate Property • Nieruchomość • Land and its immovable physical property such as improvements, landscaping and buildings. Real estate (from the Latin “res/rei” = thing) is also referred to as “real property”, “realty” or “im- movable property”. Its permanence contrasts with personal property (e.g. mov- able and depreciating things such as machinery, vehicles, money). In industrial- ized economies, real estate assets typically represent one of the largest single stores of wealth (for most households, their home is their most valuable asset; their mortgage is their largest debt). Although there is the notion of “squatter’s rights”, real estate can only fulfill its full potential value if there is an institu- tional infrastructure that assures property rights, including some kind of a sys- tem of title registration.
