Clouded Title (vernacular) • Niepewny tytuł własności (potoczne) – An uncertainty in ownership or impediment to the transfer of real estate property that diminishes its value. For example, in the absence of a survey, there could be a dispute between co-owners or neighbours. Alternatively, property ownership could be the subject of contesting interests amongst heirs. A clouded title will typically disqualify a property as mortgage security. Normally a registry system will help to establish certainty of ownership. However, a court’s ruling can be superimposed on what’s in a land titles registry. The reference to a “cloud” sug- gests lack of clarity or transparency. Contrast to clear title.
