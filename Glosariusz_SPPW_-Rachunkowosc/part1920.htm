Off-Site Controller/Comptroller • Kontroler zewnętrzny • Fulfilling the senior tasks in the accounting function externally. Professional accounting firms in many countries do this as part of their offer of non-audit services. While the requirement for independence excludes them from being auditors, many small and medium enterprises (which do not require an audit in the first place) value access to an auditor’s financial and accounting talents, neither of which they could afford or would need to hire full time.
