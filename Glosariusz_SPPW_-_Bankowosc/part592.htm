Credit adjudication

Adjudication, Credit • Przyznanie kredytu – A rather fancy (and rarely used) description of the analysis and approval of a loan proposal, typically within the formal credit process of a bank. Other descriptions would be to sanction, approve or to authorise a loan. Word roots: from the Latin “adiudico” from ad + iudico = “to judge”. The association with a court of law reflects the idea that the ‘case for’ a decision to lend is carefully weighed.
