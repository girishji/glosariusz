Jackets (trading floor) • Marynarki (parkiet) • A reference to the practice of traders in a trading pit to wear distinctive and often colourful jackets in order to help identify and find each other in a crowded, chaotic and often noisy working environment. Traders and their brokerage firms are often referred to as “jackets”.
