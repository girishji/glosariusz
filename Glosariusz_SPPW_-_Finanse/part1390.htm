Dead Cat Bounce (slang) • Podskok zdechłego kota (slang) • The intermit- tent and temporary rally of a stock that has drastically declined in value. As the stock keeps going down, some investors – bottom fishers – will buy it in the hope that the worst is over. Alas, the poor thing is dead and will never get back up.
