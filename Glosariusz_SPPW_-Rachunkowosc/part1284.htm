734 Big Four Accounting/Audit Firms‌


The share price has nowhere to go but up. It is then easier for new management to look good later on (and perhaps earn some bonuses). In its defence, accounting is supposed to be conservative, especially with bad news. What could seem like a big bath may turn out to be mere “soaking” if problems in fact prove to be worse than they at first seem. A firm that has to book subsequent write-offs after the first will lose credibility. Therefore, the amount of a large write-off is a question of judgement and finding a credible balance. Contrast to income smoothing.
