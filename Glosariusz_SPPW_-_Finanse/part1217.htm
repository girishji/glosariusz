Bond Market (Global) • Rynek obligacji (globalny) • The portion of the international capital market that trades in bonds. It is a huge market. In terms of issuers – governments, business corporations, banks, high yield, municipal and agency – it ranks highest. It also has a nominal value greater than the combined market valuations of all the world’s equity markets.
