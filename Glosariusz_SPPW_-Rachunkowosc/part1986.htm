Positive Accounting Theories • Teorie behawiorystyczne w rachunkowości •

The ideas, based on empirical study, on what effect accounting has on the behaviour and

performance of enterprises. Similar to economics, the distinction is between explanation and prediction as opposed to prescription (i.e. “normative” theories). Positive accounting theories recognise the obvious possibility that accounting is not just a neutral measure- ment tool to describe financial reality but that it can often create its own financial reality.
