Lease vs. Borrow Decision • Leasing czy kredyt – Financial leases are one way to finance the long term acquisition of fixed assets. (For a discussion of the various aspects of leases, see related entries in the Compendium of Finance). The alternative to leasing is to borrow funds directly from a lender, buy the asset in your own name and then pledge it to the lender as collateral security. Thus, banks are often competing against leasing companies in the market. In the case of smaller borrowers (e.g. SME businesses and individual consumers), the leasing alternative is often perceived as being more attractive.
