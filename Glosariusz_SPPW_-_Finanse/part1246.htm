Business Valuation • Wycena przedsiębiorstwa • The art and science of esti- mating how much a company is worth in terms of its market price (i.e. what an arm’s length buyer would pay). Relating this to financial statements using his- torical cost, business valuation converts shareholders’ equity at book value into its economic value. Business valuation has two uses. First, is when buying or selling a business (for example, a merger & acquisition). As well, the stockmarket’s valu- ation of shares is largely an exercise in valuing the business which shareholders own. Simplifying considerably, there are three basic approaches to measuring the value of an enterprise:
