Calling Officer • Inspektor ds. wizyt – A banker who specialises in prospect- ing activities, particularly in cold calling on business enterprises who are pres- ently not dealing with the bank. The calling officer function is based on three ideas. First, is the need to focus on marketing effort by designating respon- sibility to one particular individual. Often bankers with customer care duties (e.g. relationship managers, account officers) feel (rightly or wrongly) that they are too busy with existing clients to devote serious effort to selling. Second, cer- tain individuals are often better at the extroverted skills required to call on new prospects. Finally, good ‘business development’ (the banker’s euphemism for selling) comes from a ‘learning curve’ practice and training (see quote below).
