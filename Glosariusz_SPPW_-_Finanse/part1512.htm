Finance Function • Funkcja finansowa • A staff function, crossing over the core managerial activities of strategy, operations and risk management, which handles five main tasks or decisions:

◆ accounting: keeping the books, reporting, internal controls, forecasting,

◆ capital structure: finding, sourcing and sustaining appropriate levels of debt

and equity,

◆ capital budgeting: choosing capital investments, valuing projects and fore-

casting,

◆ cash (liquidity) management: default avoidance, working capital manage-

ment,

◆ tax planning: minimising, through tax avoidance but not evasion, income tax

expense.
