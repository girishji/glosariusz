Financial Accounting • Rachunkowość finansowa • The accounting that is done primarily for third party accounting users, such as shareholders, lenders and regulators. To be useful to outsiders, financial accounting must simplify and focus on key financial dis- closure items such as a balance sheet and profit and loss statement. A key concern is stew- ardship and transparency. To ensure fairness and reliability, it is governed by accounting standards and usually has the scrutiny of auditors.
