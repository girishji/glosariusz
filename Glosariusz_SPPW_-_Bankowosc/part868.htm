Hoarding (of Cash) • Gromadzenie (gotówki) – The accumulation and se- creting away of cash will occur in times of increused social or economic anxiety about the future. Another motive is lack of faith in a financial system (see mat- tress money). Roots of the word: Gaelic for “treasure”.
