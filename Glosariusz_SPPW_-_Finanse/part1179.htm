Back-of-Envelope Calculation 725‌

How does such an abuse of an otherwise sound scheme come about? Usually a conventional option plan is already in existence. Beneficiaries then surrepti- tiously change the figures in order to enrich themselves in the hope that the forgery will go unnoticed. In most jurisdictions, backdated options are consid- ered no more than a sophisticated fraud. While relatively rare, several cases of backdated stock options occurred amongst some firms in the USA’s entrepre- neurially-driven Silicon Valley until authorities clamped down on them.
