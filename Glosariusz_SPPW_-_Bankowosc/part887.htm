Judgement Creditor • Wierzyciel ustalony sądownie – A creditor who has won a claim for payment in a court of law. Being a judgement creditor allows an unsecured creditor to marshal the authority of the court to try to enforce repayment. Court-appointed bailiffs can then be empowered to take possession of the debtor’s property and sell it in order to raise proceeds to satisfy the claim. In the absence of such a sanction, it would be difficult to enforce creditor’s rights. However, a judgement on creditor’s status is still subject to priority of realisation.
