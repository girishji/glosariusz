Overweight Investment Holdings • Przeważanie aktywów w portfelu • Any investment in a particular asset class, security or category that is greater than a benchmark used to assess performance. For example, a mutual fund may proclaim to have a long run target of a certain mix of industries. But because of its bullish outlook on a certain sector, it will choose to surpass a limit or “weight” as in proportion. Underweighting reflects a bearish sentiment. By definition, index funds should be neither overweighted nor underweighted.
