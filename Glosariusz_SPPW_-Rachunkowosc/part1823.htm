Latent Liability (vernacular) • Zobowiązanie ukryte (żargon zawodowy) • An unquantifiable, potential liability that might cause a nasty surprise. It contrasts to a con- tingent liability in that an adverse event can be specifically identified (e.g. losing a law- suit). Another description might be as the risk factor of possible understated liabilities (i.e. accounting risk).



Examples of a latent liability might be a claim arising from a tax audit, a fine for regu- latory non-compliance or a sudden lawsuit. (Assuming that the entity was unable to quantity the future payment in advance.)
