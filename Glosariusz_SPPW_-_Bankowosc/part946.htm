Money Centre Banks • Banki głównego obrotu pieniężnego – Banks that operate in major financial centres. The distinction is typically made in the Unit- ed States. With its dispersed “unit banking system” over 10,000 banks, the banks in the big cities, especially New York, Chicago and Los Angeles, tended to be in a different class. With the rise, through merger, of large financial institutions

– known as “super regionals” – outside these urban centres, the distinction has less meaning.
