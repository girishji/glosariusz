Idle Time Cost • Koszt czasu przestoju • The cost of unproductive labour time caused by a lack of activity due to, say, a lack of orders, machine breakdowns, material shortages etc. Idle time is a labour variance (the difference between hours worked and paid at stan- dard (budgeted) wage rate). Under responsibility accounting systems, it is charged to the cost centre that caused the problem.
