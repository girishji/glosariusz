Stocks (USA) • Akcje • Udziały • The US equivalent of equity shares. In the UK, “stock” refers to inventory while “loan stock” refers to traded debt securities. While the words are synonymous they are not used interchangeably. From stock we get stock market (but not “share market”), stock broker (but not “share bro- ker”) and stock exchange (but not “share exchange”). Word roots – uncertain (www.etymonline.com); perhaps comes from “trunk” from which gains are an outgrowth or a “money-box” (c.1400). Meaning “subscribed capital of a corpo- ration” goes back to 1612.
