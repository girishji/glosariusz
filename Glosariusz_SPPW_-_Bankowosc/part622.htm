Bad Loan • Dług zły – A risk categorisation of a loan whose value has been signifi- cantly impaired with a likelihood of less than full repayment and the consequent need to recognise a loss. It is said that no prudent banker [purposely] makes a bad loan. But ill-advised loans may be poorly thought out and be fraught with trouble from the first advance.
