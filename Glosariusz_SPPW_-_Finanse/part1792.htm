Matching Principle (Financial) • Zasada dopasowania • The convention of harmonising the characteristics of financing sources to the assets that are be- ing financed. Since it is the asset conversion and/or cash flow from assets that must fulfil the obligations of financing sources (both liabilities and equity capi- tal), it stands to reason that there should be a matching or equivalence between them. Ideally, financing sources should mirror the various attributes of the as- sets at work:
