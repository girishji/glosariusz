What-if Scenarios (vernacular) • Scenariusz „co, jeśli” (żargon) • A finan- cial planning and investment tool that looks at a company’s forecasted perfor- mance under various assumptions. Thus, we ask: “What if sales fell by 15%, what would be the impact on profits or cash flow…?” The exercise is a useful way to gauge commercial risk and is made feasible by powerful spreadsheet tools.



Where Are the Customers’ Yachts? (rhetorical question) • Gdzie są jachty klientów? (pytanie retoryczne) • A thought provocation based on

the title of a popular and enduring book first published in 1940. The author Fred Schwed was a successful professional trader who made his fortune in 1929 just before the Great Crash. Cashing out, he then retired from Wall Street to write. Penned in a cheeky style, the book has been called a “hilarious classic” for its irreverent look into the inner workings of finance capitalism.
