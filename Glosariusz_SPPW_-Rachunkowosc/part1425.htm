Cook the Books (slang) • Fałszować (preparować) księgi (żargon zawodowy) •

To purposely manipulate (as in changing a recipe) the financial statements (as in books of

account) in order to distort the truth. The term has a joking, tongue’n’cheek connotation with an imprecise idea of somehow intervening in financial reality. We can look at this effort in the context of a spectrum ranging from “the good; the bad & the ugly”.
