Kaizen Budgeting (Japan) • Budżetowanie według filozofii Kaizen (Japonia) •

A system of continuous incremental improvement (say, 1–5%/year) in standard costs,

performance or quality. While never being satisfied, such self-inflicted pressure ensures competitiveness. In intensely competitive markets where technology is often allow- ing efficiency gains, the spirit of Kaizen ensures all opportunities are either explored or exploited. See also zero based budgeting.
