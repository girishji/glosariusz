Locusts (slang) • Szarańcza kapitałowa (slang) • A famous derogatory and vivid description of US and UK hedge funds in Germany rendered in 2005 by Mr Franz Muntefering, a Social Democrat, who became Germany’s deputy chancellor. Mr Muntefering was referring to the activist stance of foreign funds putting shareholder value-creation performance pressure on the incumbent managements of domestic German companies. The comment was not only xenophobic but misguided if we acknowledge the existence of the agency prob- lem and the need for a healthy dose of pressure on management.
