Scenario Analysis • Analiza scenariuszy • The calculation of several outcomes (e.g. “worst case”, “expected case”, “best case”) based on varying input assump- tions. For example, a cash flow forecast might have multiple outcomes based on a range of sales volumes, costs or interest rates, etc. If the user is confident about the inputs, a range of outcomes can be defined. Scenario analysis is an impor- tant technique in risk management by helping to appreciate the answer to “what if?” queries.
