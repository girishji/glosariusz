896 Extrinsic Value of Options‌

Extrinsic Value of Options • Time Value of Options • Wartość ze- wnętrzna (wartość czasowa) opcji • The amount by which an option’s price‌

exceeds its intrinsic value (i.e. the difference between the market price and the exercise price when the option is in the money; when out of the money, intrinsic value is nil). Extrinsic value represents the valuation of the possibility that the market price could rise sometime within the time horizon of the option’s matu- rity. The valuation is complex. See option pricing model.
