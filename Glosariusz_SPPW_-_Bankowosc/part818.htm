Events of Default • Przypadki naruszenia umowy – A specification of default that in turn defines when a lender can exercise legal remedies such as demanding repayment and realising on collateral security (see quote below). The specification of such events are negotiated and incor- porated into a loan contract. Some of the most typical events of default would include:

◆ inaccurate warranties (false representations are not a breach of contract)

◆ failure to pay interest or principal when due

◆ failure to fulfil a covenant such as a financial test

◆ bankruptcy, liquidation or appointment of a receiver

◆ a court judgement in excess of a specified threshold

◆ impairment of pledged asset

◆ cancellation of a supporting guarantee

◆ elimination of collateral security
