It’s very, very, very hard to regulate people. If I were appointed a new regulator – if you gave me 100 of the smartest people you can imagine to work for me, and every day I got the positions from the biggest institutions, all their derivative positions, all their stock positions and currency positions, I wouldn’t be able to tell you how they were doing. It’s very, very hard to regulate when you’ve got hundreds of counterparties…[While] regulating is an important part of the system, the efficacy of it is really tough.

Warren Buffett on the inherent difficulties of financial regulation quoted

What Warren Thinks in“Fortune”, April 28, 2008, p. 27

Reinsurance • Reasekuracja • The coverage by one insurance company (the “reinsurer”) of another insurance company in exchange for a share of the pre- mium paid. It is analogous to syndicated loans amongst banks to spread risks.
