Preserve Capital (vernacular) • Ochrona kapitału (żargon) • A reference to protecting wealth from any future erosion due to inflation or investment loss. This means avoiding speculative investing. Such caution will be sought even if potential rewards must be forfeited. Thus we might say: “Prior to their retirement, investors should refrain from seeking growth and instead concen- trate on preserving capital so that they can be certain about meeting their life- style needs”. Capital preservation is also an objective in difficult market condi- tions (see below).
