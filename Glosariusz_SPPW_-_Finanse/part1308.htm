Churning (slang) • Zbieranie śmietanki (slang) • Purposely encouraging – usually by investment recommendations – a high volume of buys and sells in a stock securities portfolio. Since a brokerage business generates revenue from trading commissions, it tends to profit more from customers who churn as op- posed to those with a patient “buy and hold” strategy. The difference between churning and a more legitimate profit taking is one of degree and balance.
