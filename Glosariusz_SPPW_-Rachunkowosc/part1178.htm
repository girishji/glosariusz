Accounting Records • Dokumentacja księgowa • A broad term denoting all the vari- ous internal documentation necessary to handle accounting activities. This would include the books of account, time sheets, manuals and customer records in both hard copy and electronic versions of computerised records. Accounting records are utilised for both internal management accounting and external financial reporting purposes.
