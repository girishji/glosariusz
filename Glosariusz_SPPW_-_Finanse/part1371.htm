828 Coverage Ratio (Financial Ratio)‌



Coverage Ratio (Financial Ratio) • Wskaźnik pokrycia (wskaźnik fi- nansowy) • A type of financial ratio that focuses on annual flow relationships.

Three common ratios and their (admittedly rough) benchmarks are:


Coverage Ratio

Ratio

Formulation

Benchmark

“Interest Coverage”

Coverage ratios make the most sense when looking at the relationship between two causally related components, one of which depends on the other. For exam- ple, since interest is paid from ongoing profitability, the two flows should be compared to understand how reliable interest payments will be in the face of possible changes in profits.
