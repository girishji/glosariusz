Interest rate risk can be reduced by using fixed rate debt or employing hedging instruments such as swaps. Because of the adverse impact on borrower debt ser- vice capacity, banks will generally react to higher interest rates by lending less. This is the prime mechanism which makes interest rates a monetary policy tool to “cool down” or “heat up” an economy.
