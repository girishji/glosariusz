Exotics (slang) • Opcje egzotyczne (slang) • Unusual or rare financial instru- ments that are complex. Typically designed for special situations, exotics are only limited by the imagination and needs of their users. In mature markets, the focus is shifting to problem solving capacity, transparency and suitability vs. purposeful sophistication.
