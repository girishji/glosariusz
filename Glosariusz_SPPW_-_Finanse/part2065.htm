Securities Arbitration (USA) • Postępowanie arbitrażowe (w USA) • A dispute resolution mechanism in the securities industry. Most securities firms in the USA get advance written agreement from their clients that any disputes or complaints will be submitted to an arbitration process governed by the Na- tional Association of Securities Dealers (NASD). This is in lieu of going through the slower and more costly court system, although clients still have this ultimate legal sanction.
