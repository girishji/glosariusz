726 Backwardation‌‌‌

Backwardation • Deport • Transakcja deportowa • When a commodity is worth more in today’s spot market than in its futures market. Normally, the fu- tures price is greater than the spot (a situation known as contango). However, if the markets expect the price of the commodity to fall over time, the future price might be less than today’s spot.
