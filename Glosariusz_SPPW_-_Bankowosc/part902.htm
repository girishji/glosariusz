Liar Loan (US slang) • Kredyt na oświadczenie (slang amerykański) – A loan granted on such liberal and unsubstantiated terms that its underlying creditworthiness was nothing but a ‘pack of lies’. For instance, if lenders are willing to lend primarily based on the collateral security being pledged, they may not bother confirming, or even care about, such conventional qualifying crite- ria as a satisfactory credit history, financial status and repayment capacity etc. The term is associated with the sub-prime mortgages that were used to populate securities that came to be described as toxic assets.
