Drip-Feeding (slang) • Zasilanie kropelkowe (slang) • The venture capital- ist’s practice of gradually injecting cash into a business start-up. The money is provided slowly but surely for several reasons. First, is to avoid profligacy from access to too much money. Second, is to give entrepreneurs incentive to keep moving forward by executing business plans. And finally, drip-feeding limits exposure in those instances where the start-up is abandoned.
