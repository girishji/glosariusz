Net Worth • Wartość netto przedsiębiorstwa • The value of total assets less or net of associated liabilities. Net worth tends to be synonymous with net asset value, sharehold- er’s equity or net book value. If there is a distinction, it is the subtlety implied by the term “worth”: the value is not necessarily confined to book values.

Non-Consolidated Subsidiary • Jednostka zależna nie objęta konsolidacją

• A subsidiary that, despite normal consolidation accounting procedures, is not consoli-

dated line-by-line into its parent’s accounts (rather, it is shown as a single investment item

using the equity method). Most accounting standards and/or corporate laws will tolerate this as an exception based on the following situations:
