Net present value is the aggregation of all cash flows, both positive and negative. Calculating a present value in effect eliminates the impact of the time value of money. If a quantification of risk can be embedded in the discount rate, rational investment decisions can then be made based on a time and risk neutral cost- benefit analysis. Present value is one of the most important and common calcu- lations in finance. See capital (expenditure) budgeting process.
