Burn Rate (slang) • Wskaźnik nakładów gotówkowych (slang) • The amount of funds required per period (usually a year) to cover operating deficits in a new start-up enterprise, particularly if it is a high technology company which is spending on the research and development of new products. The burn rate is usually financed by venture capitalists.

generated by a commercial activity or investment. The word “case” is used in the sense of putting forward an argument that can be scrutinised and challenged, as if the courtroom. Thus it includes not just a budget or a forecast but the economic rationale behind the assumptions.
