COMR is a useful external monitoring tool allowing comparisons both over time (re: trends) and between peers (re: benchmarks). It suggests that there is a logical connection between the rewards given to the top management team and the results enjoyed by shareholders. Given the increasing hard-nosed scrutiny of banker’s compensation, the COMR is an important metric for bank stakeholders.
