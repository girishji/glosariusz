The treasury stock method assumes that the proceeds from selling treasury shares at the warrant’s or option’s exercise price are used to buy and cancel traded shares in the market that have been purchased at market price. Any shares that cannot be purchased are a “residual” that makes the warrants/op- tions dilutive.
