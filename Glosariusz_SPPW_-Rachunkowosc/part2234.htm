Unadjusted Differences • Nieskorygowane różnice • Accounting errors discov- ered by an auditor that are not yet put through the books because they are either being disputed by management or are perhaps immaterial. As well, the net effect of cumulative material unadjusted errors may well offset amongst themselves to become immaterial.

Unaudited Numbers • Niebadane sprawozdanie finansowe • Financial state- ments that have not received the benefit of an audit opinion. This would typically include interim statements and statements issued by a company to private persons, perhaps prior to the completion of an audit (which may take 2–5 months after the year-end).
