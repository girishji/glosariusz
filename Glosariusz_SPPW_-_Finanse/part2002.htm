The wealthy persons (“rentiers”) who lived off their interest income went from riches to rags planting a traditional French aversion towards financial markets. See also gilt security.

Repatriate Earnings • Transfer zysków • To bring earnings – or more accu- rately cash flow – back to a home country market from an offshore investment. A multinational corporation, for example, will have foreign subsidiaries in other countries. Because the subsidiaries will be consolidated into the parent’s finan- cial statements, their income will be a part of the group’s, regardless of whether the earnings stay in the foreign country or not. If the subsidiary has attractive local investment opportunities, it might reinvest its own earnings.
