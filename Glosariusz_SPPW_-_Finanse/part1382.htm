Dabble in the Market • Spekulować (dosłownie: pluskać się w rynku) • A stock phrase meaning to play the market in a casual, amateurish, frivolous or naive way. The root meaning of the word is to splash liquid gently and play- fully. It thus connotes something done superficially without serious intent.
