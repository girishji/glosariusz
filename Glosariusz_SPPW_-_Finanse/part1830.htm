The calculation of NPV involves the modelling of all relevant cash flows for a project. This would include capturing such metrics as the initial cash outlay (e.g. the project capital expenditure) and project future cash flows (e.g. operat- ing cash flows, opportunity costs, income taxes, inflation and working capi- tal). Netting (Settlement and Clearing) • Kompensowanie należności • The clearinghouse practice of only transferring cash after all a period’s payments have been recorded, many of which will offset each other thus reducing the amount and frequency of funds transfer.

Neuer Market • Neuer Markt • The German stock market segment for young, fast growing companies. It is a similar market to America’s NASDAQ. Other equivalents are Nouveau Marché (France) and Japan’s NASDAQ. The complete collapse of the high-tech sector in late 2001 led to the Neuer Market’s closure in 2003.
