Just-In-Time (JIT) Inventory Management • Zarządzanie zapasami w systemie „dokładnie na czas” • Accelerating the delivery, processing and

shipment of physical goods in order to reduce the resources being tied up in inventory to an absolute minimum. JIT utilizes ERP, outsourcing, information technology and electronic data interchange with suppliers to ensure a very mod- est “transaction motive” (i.e. need to have stock on hand to fill production scheduling gaps). Under JIT, inventory represents less of an asset than a measure
