988 Lower-of-Cost-or-Market Valuation Method‌‌


Lower-of-Cost-or-Market Valuation Method • LOCOM Valuation Method

• Metoda wyceny zapasów po koszcie nie przekraczającym wartości ryn- kowej • A carrying amount of an asset on the balance sheet that recognised unrealised

losses and gains in market value below the original purchase price (which forms a ceiling on value). Any unrealised gains or losses above historical costs are ignored. LOCOM is a popular valuation method in held-to-maturity investments. See also historical cost carry- ing amounts and fair value carrying amounts.
