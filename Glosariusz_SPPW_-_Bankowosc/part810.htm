Earning Assets Ratio • Wskaźnik aktywów dochodowych banku – The ra- tio of assets of a bank that are generating cash revenue in the short term com- pared to the bank’s total assets. For most commercial banks their primary earn- ing assets are loan and securities portfolios. Other, non-earning assets may have a long-term purpose. For example, cash reserves are necessitated by regulatory re- quirements and are an unavoidable cost of doing business. Or, a branch network may allow deposits to be gathered at low interest cost which is necessary to pro- vide a healthy net interest margin. However, in the short term, bank management needs to focus on generating a sufficient yield on earning assets to carry the dead weight burden of non-earning assets.
