c/fwd or c/f (Bookkeeping symbols) • Sk. (skrót księgowy) • Shorthand for “carried forward”, a balancing entry in a ledger account. See balancing the books.

C’s of Accounting (English language mnemonic) • Cechy rachunkowości

(po angielsku wszystkie na literę C) • The characteristics best suited to financial

accountants and auditors:

◆ careful,

◆ cautious,

◆ conservative,

◆ consistent,

◆ correct,

◆ conscientious.

Thus accounting people are not expected to be sharp operators or “wheeler dealers”. See also prudential accounting concept.
